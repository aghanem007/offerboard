Contact

Questions, bug reports, or a mod situation that needs eyes.

Something broken?

Offerboard is an open-source project. If you hit a bug, the fastest route is opening an issue on the GitHub repository — include what you did, what you expected, and what actually happened.

Community issues

See something that breaks the house rules? Moderators patrol the board daily. Post in General and tag the situation, or reach out to any moderator directly — they're marked with a blue Mod badge.

Everything else

For anything that doesn't fit above, start a topic in General. The community is faster than any support inbox.
