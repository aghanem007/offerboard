Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Overview

We want you to be satisfied with your VIP membership purchase. This policy outlines when and how you can request a refund for Pixel VIP access, which includes private builds, plugin packs, telemetry tools, and priority support.

Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • You request a refund within 7 days of your purchase
  • You have not downloaded private builds or plugin packs
  • There is a technical issue with the software that we cannot resolve

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Requests made after the 7-day period
  • Accounts that have been banned for policy violations or safety gate tampering
  • Change of mind after downloading VIP builds or plugin packs
  • Issues caused by your own system configuration, display settings, or calibration
  • Detection or OCR accuracy issues related to your specific setup
  • Third-party software or application conflicts

How to Request a Refund

  1. Log into your forum account
  2. Go to Support
  3. Create a new support request
  4. Select "Billing" as the department
  5. Provide your order details, account email, and reason for the refund

Processing Time

Refund requests are typically processed within 3-5 business days. Once approved, the refund will be credited to your original payment method within 5-10 business days, depending on your payment provider. VIP access will be revoked upon refund approval.

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer a partial refund based on the extent of service used (e.g., builds downloaded, support tickets resolved). This is determined on a case-by-case basis.

Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback without first contacting us for a refund, your account will be permanently suspended and VIP access revoked. We encourage you to reach out to our support team first to resolve any issues.

Contact

For refund inquiries, please use our Support system or visit the Contact page.
