Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password when you register
  • Profile Information: Any additional information you add to your forum profile
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through our payment providers; we do not store card details
  • Support Communications: Tickets, messages, and attachments you send through our support system
  • Forum Content: Posts, replies, and other content you publish on the forums

2. Information Collected Automatically

When you use our website and forum, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information and operating system
  • Pages visited, access times, and session duration
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. Local Telemetry Data

The Pixel automation framework generates telemetry data locally on your machine. This data is important to understand:

  • What is logged: Detection events, policy decisions, safety gate results, input actions, and session metadata are recorded in JSONL format on your local machine
  • Where it stays: Telemetry logs are stored locally in your logs/ directory. We do not collect, transmit, or access your telemetry data
  • Screen capture data: Pixel captures screen regions for detection and OCR processing. These captures are processed in memory and are not stored or transmitted
  • Profile configurations: Your profiles, calibration data, and plugin settings are stored locally and are not sent to our servers

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our website and forum services
  • Process VIP membership transactions and send related information
  • Deliver software updates and build downloads to eligible members
  • Send technical notices, support responses, and service announcements
  • Respond to your support tickets and questions
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized account sharing

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With service providers who assist in our operations (hosting, payment processing)
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, safety, and the integrity of our services
  • With your consent

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including encrypted connections and secure authentication. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your forum posts and support history
  • Opt out of marketing communications

8. Cookies

We use cookies to manage authentication sessions and enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser, though disabling cookies may affect forum login functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the date above.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our Contact page or submit a request through our Support system.
