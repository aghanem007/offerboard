Privacy Policy

What we collect, why, and what we'll never do with it.

What we collect

  • Account data: your email address, display name, and password (hashed by Supabase Auth — we never see it).
  • Content: topics, replies, follows, and warnings tied to your account.
  • Activity: a last-seen timestamp that powers the "who's online" widget.

What we don't collect

No trackers, no analytics pixels, no ad networks, no selling data. Offerboard is a student project, not a data business.

Where it lives

Data is stored in a Supabase (PostgreSQL) project with row-level security enforcing who can read and write what. Your email is never displayed publicly — only your display name appears on the board.

Salary posts and anonymity

Compensation threads are public. If you post numbers, post only what you're comfortable having tied to your display name. Pick an anonymous display name if that matters to you — plenty of members do.

Deleting your data

Ask via the contact page and your account and its content will be removed. Moderation records may be retained in anonymized form.

Changes

If this policy changes meaningfully, it'll be announced on the board first.
