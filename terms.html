Terms of Use

The short version: be useful, be honest, don't be a jerk.

1. What Offerboard is

Offerboard is a community forum where CS students share interview experiences, compensation data, resume feedback, and prep advice. It's peer knowledge, not professional advice — verify anything important before betting your career on it.

2. Your account

You're responsible for what happens under your account. Keep your password to yourself, pick a display name you're not embarrassed by, and don't impersonate other people or companies.

3. House rules

  • Post real information. Fabricated offers and made-up interview stories poison the well for everyone.
  • Roast resumes, not people. Feedback is the point; cruelty isn't.
  • Respect confidentiality. Don't name individual interviewers, and honor any NDA you've signed.
  • No spam. No recruiting posts, referral farming, course shilling, or self-promo without substance.
  • Nothing illegal or hateful. Obvious, but here it is in writing.

4. Moderation

Moderators can remove content, issue warnings, and suspend accounts. Three warnings triggers an automatic ban. Moderation calls are made in good faith and are final — arguing in bad faith is itself a good way to collect warning number two.

5. Your content

You own what you post. By posting, you give Offerboard a license to display it here. Removed content may be retained in moderated form for audit purposes.

6. No warranty

Offerboard is provided as-is, run by students, for free. We make no guarantees about uptime, accuracy of posted salaries, or whether that one comment about your resume was fair.

7. Changes

These terms may evolve as the community does. Significant changes will be announced on the board.
